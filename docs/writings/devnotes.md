### ADB

```js
- adb devices
- start-server
- adb kill-server
- adb tcpip 5555 
// to enable connecting to phone through 5555 port
- adb connect 100.121.153.58:5555 
// ip address of phone, to connect wirelessly
```

- `:q` (quit)
